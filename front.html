How it works

Car Damage Detection allows you to upload a picture of your car damage to indepently assess damage location and severity, easing the burden of filing an insurance claim and getting your car repaired.


SPAN

Take a picture of your car damage. For best results, try to focus on one major area of damage (e.g., front, side, or rear) and contain most of the car body in the picture.

SUBMIT

Upload your picture to our car damage detection system testing its capabilities.

ASSESS

Convolutional neural networks will identify the location of where the damage has been done and what is the severity of damage that has been done.

RESULTS

Results can be used to help a customer make an insurance claim and to help customer get thier car repaired.
